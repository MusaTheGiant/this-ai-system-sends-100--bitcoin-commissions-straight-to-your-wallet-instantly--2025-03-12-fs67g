No. You can earn without recruiting, but those who share their affiliate link tend to earn faster.

As an affiliate, you earn 100% instant commissions in Bitcoin when customers use your referral link to purchase any Credit Package, ranging from the $25 package to the $8,000 package. There are no withdrawal delays. Bitcoin is sent instantly and directly to your Bitcoin wallet. You do not even need to request a withdrawal

Is this program legit?

Yes, this program is legitimate. It offers real AI products and marketing tools, backed by a transparent and trustworthy company.

No, this is an Affiliate Marketing company. You promote and sell AI-powered marketing tools using a unique link, earning commissions when someone makes a purchase through your link.
